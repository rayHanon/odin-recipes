Garlic Butter Shrimp

Description

This quick and easy garlic butter shrimp recipe is perfect for busy weeknights. The shrimp is sautéed in a rich garlic butter sauce with a splash of lemon and parsley.
Serve it over rice, pasta, or with a slice of crusty bread to soak up the sauce. It’s flavorful, fast, and satisfying!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat until melted and bubbling.
  3. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  4. Add the shrimp in a single layer and cook for 2-3 minutes on one side.
  5. Flip the shrimp and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until pink and opaque.
  6. Squeeze in the lemon juice and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
  7. Remove from heat and serve immediately with your favorite side.
